The House


The House is a mysterious building home to the secluded Estrada Family, and its features are as baffling as its demeanor. Random doors locked, windows unable to be opened, and lights that stop working after dark keep people out - and keep others in. Perhaps this house isn’t what it pretends to be.



       




While trying to find a way to escape the confines of The House, Minerva notices a piece of paper tucked haphazardly at the back of a kitchen cabinet.

She pulls it from its hiding place and unfolds it, immediately dumbfounded by its contents.

Minerva scoffs as she shoves the page into her pocket. So much for finding anything useful.
