When Minerva finally got the basement door open, she was confused to find not a room full of old boxes and the breaker, but instead a dark blue hallway lit only by the light coming from a frosty glass door at the end of it.

She tried the handle, but it was locked. Beside the door was a keypad, five dim leds stationed above the number keys. Minerva pushed a few random buttons, lighting up the lights as she went, until they beamed red and shut back off. Input password?

The lab lies dormant beneath the Estrada house, revealing that the home was nothing but a facade. Built by Dr. Moore for an experiment, The Lab is in shambles when found; scientists missing, broken objects scattered everywhere, and most importantly- a plain white room with a big white machine ripped apart by its hinges. What happened here?
