Story


Maria was a normal girl, albeit very sickly. Her parents feared she wouldn’t live for long, and in their time of mourning and desperation, a miracle came to them; a scientist who promised she could bring back their sweet little girl. But Maria was still very much alive. And she won’t go without a fight.


So when Minerva wakes up in a house that isn’t hers, to parents who claim she’s their daughter when she’s never met them in her life, it's up to her to figure out how she got their, how to get out, what happened to the girl before her, and more importantly, what the aggressive shadow lurking at night is.


DAUGHTER is a third person adventure puzzle horror game that explores alternate realities, guilt and haterade, and traumatic stories. Play as Minerva, trapped in an empty house with entities both friendly and not as she stumbles upon secrets left to be uncovered.
